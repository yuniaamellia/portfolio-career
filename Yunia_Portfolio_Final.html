— KOL · Viral Strategy 2021

Sisca Kohl × Evangeline Perfume

KOL Strategist · Freelance

The brief was simple: make people aware of Evangeline as a local perfume brand. But on TikTok, ordinary content drowns. We chose Sisca Kohl — her aesthetic, her audience, her pull.

What made this campaign special wasn't just the reach. It was the conversion proof that lived in the comments. The video went viral, but more importantly: people commented saying they bought the perfume because of the video. Strangers tagged friends. Reviews followed. Comments became social proof, which became more sales.

— KOL · Strategy & Efficiency 2023

Grab × OVO × 3MONGKIS × Fore Coffee

KOL Strategist · Rekreasi Creative Playground

The challenge: pitch influencers without rate card budget for a Halal Bihalal & Trunk Show activation. The solution: trade in non-financial value — networking, exposure, outfit barter. Personal pitching. The kind that makes KOLs feel like it's an opportunity, not just an invitation.

Many influencers participated because they saw the value beyond money. KOLs who only received allowance and outfits ended up generating more reach than the paid ones.
